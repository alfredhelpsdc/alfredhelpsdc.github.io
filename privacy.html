Privacy Policy

How We Handle Your Data

1. Information We Collect

a) Message Data

  • Up to 140 messages per channel for conversation context
  • Message content, timestamps, and associated metadata

b) User Information

  • Discord user ID
  • Display name
  • Server-specific settings
  • Usage statistics and message counts
  • Achievement and badge data
  • User behavior analysis data

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide AI-powered conversation capabilities
  • To maintain context in ongoing discussions
  • To improve our services and AI models
  • To personalize your experience

3. Data Protection

  • Industry-standard encryption for data transmission
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limited access to personal information

4. Your Rights

  • Request data export
  • Delete your data
  • Opt-out of data collection
