How We Handle Your Data

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 23, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Alfred ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects your data when you use our Discord bot. We are committed to ensuring the privacy and security of your information.

2. Information We Collect

When you use Alfred, we may collect the following information:

  • User Data: Discord user ID
  • Server Data: Server ID and channel IDs where Alfred is used
  • Conversation Data: Messages sent to Alfred and AlfredHelps's responses
  • Settings: User and server preferences configured through Alfred

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Providing and maintaining AlfredHelps's services
  • Improving and developing new features
  • Analyzing usage patterns to optimize performance
  • Ensuring compliance with our Terms of Service
  • Responding to user support requests

4. Data Storage and Security

We store your data on secure servers and implement appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Data Retention

We retain conversation data for up to 30 days to maintain context for ongoing conversations.

6. Third-Party Services

Alfred may use third-party services to enhance functionality, such as:

  • AI providers for language processing and image generation

These third parties may have access to your data only to perform specific tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to processing of your data
  • Request restriction of processing

8. Children's Privacy

Alfred is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13, or the minimum age required by Discord in your country. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any significant changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our Discord support server.
